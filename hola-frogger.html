Hola Frogger is designed and developed to help

conserve the endangered species.

About the Project

Have you ever considered the possibility of not having crops grown or outbreak of deadly diseases due to water pollution in Australia?
Do you know that frogs play an important role in our evolution and keep all of them in under control?

Frogs play a major role in every aspect of human life which is why our application wants to bring change and create value to our ecosystem.

Frogs are the most critical species in our food chain. Not only it enables us to grow crops but also keeps the insect population on the check.Over the last two decades, more than 20 million frogs have died due to increasing human activity leading to loss of its habitat. Needless to say, few people are out there who are trying to protect this species from getting extinct but there is a lack of technology in the scheme of things. The use of primitive ways to locate frogs and finding ways to conserve them is still being widely used.

Hola Frogger uses our cutting-edge machine learning technology to identify and locate frogs across Victoria enabling enthusiast and wildlife conservationists to use their mobile phones and change the way they interact with frogs in the wild. Our application is equipped with the first of its kind lens feature which can identify frogs using our core ML data even when the device is offline. We have also incorporated challenges for the user to visit new species with location markers across Victoria to engage the user on our application. To aid them with the location our application provides weather data to increase chances of frog sightings. This will, in turn, bring huge value for the novice user and increase the community conserving frogs. Additionally, our application provides curated articles from across the web related to frogs to let the user stay up to date with the latest updates.
